Unpopular opinion: transitioning from SAP to a modern Business Intelligence (BI) solution is a make-or-break moment for mid-market companies. 
   
Most companies struggle to unlock the full potential of their SAP data, relying on manual reporting and spreadsheet chaos. This leads to data silos, lack of real-time dashboards, and ERP data locked away from key stakeholders.

To overcome this, we recommend a step-by-step approach:
1. **Assess your current state**: Take stock of your existing SAP infrastructure, data sources, and reporting requirements.
2. **Define your BI vision**: Identify the key performance indicators (KPIs) and metrics that matter most to your business, and design a tailored BI roadmap.
3. **Choose the right tools**: Select a modern BI platform that integrates seamlessly with SAP, such as Power BI or similar solutions, and supports self-service analytics and real-time data visualization.
